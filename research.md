<section class="page-intro">
  <h1>Research</h1>
  <p>
    My research interests are in photonics, optical neural networks, and inverse design.
    I am especially interested in how physical systems can be used for computation,
    control, and efficient information processing.
  </p>
</section>

    <div class="item">
